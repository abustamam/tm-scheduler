## Findings considered and rejected

- **Stubbed magic-link email sender** (`src/lib/auth.ts:11-15`): not a finding —
  it's a documented, already-tracked pre-launch task (ADR-0004) and the code
  matches the ADR. (It IS the dependency that gates plan 010's build.)
- **Unused `notifications` table** (`src/db/schema.ts:179-191`): by design per
  `CONTEXT.md:38` ("schema must not block, build no logic"). Becomes the
  substrate for plan 010, not a debt item.
- **Two import aliases `#/*` and `@/*`**: documented in `CLAUDE.md`; `@/*` exists
  for shadcn's `components.json`. Intentional, not debt.
- **No N+1 / no perf finding**: all three read queries are single grouped/joined
  statements with appropriate indexes; nothing to do at this scale.
- **`db:seed.ts` duplicate slot-generation logic**: real minor duplication, but
  deliberately left out of plan 001's scope to keep that refactor DB-free and
  low-risk. A future cleanup can point seed at `generateSlotRows`. Low leverage.
