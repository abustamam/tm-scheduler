New to Toastmasters? The Roles, Explained
Toastmaster — Hosts the meeting, keeps it moving and introduces every segment and speaker.
General Evaluator — Leads the evaluation team and reviews how the whole meeting ran.
Prepared Speaker — Delivers a 5–7 minute speech from their Pathways learning project.
Evaluator — Offers warm, specific feedback to help a speaker grow.
Table Topics Master — Runs the fun, impromptu 1–2 minute speaking session.
Grammarian — Shares the Word of the Day and notes memorable language used.
Ah-Counter — Gently tracks filler words ("um," "ah," "so") to sharpen delivery.
Timer — Flashes green, amber & red so every speaker stays on time.
Our mission — to build a thriving community that fosters self-development and leadership through dynamic learning experiences.
